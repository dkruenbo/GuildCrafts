========================================
  GuildCrafts — User Guide
========================================


WHAT IS GUILDCRAFTS?
--------------------
A recipe book for your entire guild. Instead of whispering
every guildie asking "Can anyone craft Flask of Supreme Power?"
you open one window and instantly see every recipe that every
guild member knows, across all crafting professions.

Browse by profession, search by item name, and send craft
requests directly to online players — all from one UI.


GETTING STARTED
---------------
1. Copy the GuildCrafts folder into your WoW addons directory:
     World of Warcraft/_classic_/Interface/AddOns/GuildCrafts

2. Restart WoW (or type /reload).

3. Open each of your profession windows once (e.g. click your
   Alchemy skill). The addon scans your recipes at that moment.

4. Done. From now on, your recipes sync automatically with
   every guildie who has the addon installed.

Note: The addon does NOT read your recipes on its own. It only
scans when you open a profession window. If you see "0 recipes"
for yourself, just open that profession once.

Enchanting works the same way from your perspective, even though
WoW treats it differently under the hood.


THE MAIN WINDOW
---------------
Type /gc to open or close the window. You can also click the
minimap button (see below).

The window has two panels:

  LEFT PANEL                         RIGHT PANEL
  ─────────                          ───────────
  Browse professions → members       Recipes, search results,
  → recipe lists                     or craft details

  Alchemy          (3)               ┌─ Felsteel Longblade ──────┐
  Blacksmithing    (5)               │  Source: Trainer           │
  Enchanting       (2)               │  Reagents: 6x Fel Iron... │
  Engineering      (1)               │  Category: Weapons        │
  Jewelcrafting    (4)               └────────────────────────────┘
  Leatherworking   (2)
  Tailoring        (3)

Click a profession → see members who have it.
Click a member → see their recipes.

SEARCH BAR (top of window)
  Type any item name to search across ALL guild members.
  Results show who can craft it, online status, and a
  "Request Craft" button for online players.

SYNC DOT (top-right corner)
  Green  = fully synced
  Yellow = sync in progress
  Red    = no connection / alone
  Hover for details (last sync time, DR identity, user count).

CRAFT QUEUE BAR (bottom)
  Shows pending craft requests you have accepted.
  Click "Done" when finished crafting.


CRAFT REQUESTS
--------------
Found a recipe you need? Click "Request Craft" next to an
online crafter's name.

  → The crafter gets a popup: Accept or Decline.
  → If accepted, the item appears in their craft queue.
  → When done, they click "Done" and you get a notification.
  → If the crafter doesn't have the addon, a regular whisper
    is sent instead.


WHAT YOU SEE IN THE RECIPE VIEW
-------------------------------
When browsing a member's recipe list, each entry shows:

  REAGENTS
    Materials needed (e.g. "2x Fel Lotus, 1x Mana Thistle").
    Lets you gather mats before requesting a craft.

  CATEGORIES
    Recipes are grouped by type — Potions, Elixirs, Flasks
    for Alchemy; Weapons, Armor for Blacksmithing, etc.
    Sorted alphabetically within each group.

  SKILL LEVEL
    Each member's skill (e.g. "375/375") appears next to their
    name. Tells you if they can learn high-level recipes they
    don't have yet.

  SPECIALISATION
    TBC profession specs are shown next to the member's name
    (e.g. Transmutation Master, Mooncloth Tailoring, Goblin
    Engineer). Useful for spec-exclusive recipes and procs.

  COOLDOWNS
    Timed recipes (Transmutes, Primal Mooncloth, Spellcloth,
    Shadowcloth) show time remaining (e.g. "1d 12h remaining").
    Syncs to the guild so raid leaders can plan.

  STALE DATA
    If a member's data is 30+ days old, a red tag appears
    (e.g. [30d ago] or [2mo ago]). Means they haven't opened
    their profession window with the addon in a while.

  PROFESSION ICONS
    Each profession shows its WoW icon (mortar-and-pestle for
    Alchemy, anvil for Blacksmithing, etc.) for quick scanning.


TOOLTIPS
--------
Hover over any item anywhere in the game — bags, auction house,
chat links — and the tooltip shows which guild members can craft
it. Online crafters in green, offline in grey.


MINIMAP BUTTON
--------------
A small book icon on your minimap. Left-click to toggle the
window. Drag to reposition. Type /gc minimap to hide or show it.
Position is saved between sessions.


HOW SYNCING WORKS (BEHIND THE SCENES)
--------------------------------------
You don't need to do anything — this all happens automatically.

  1. LOGIN
     Your addon says "hello" to the guild and requests any
     recipe data you're missing.

  2. COORDINATOR (DR)
     One addon user is automatically elected as the Designated
     Router — the central sync point. A Backup (BDR) stands by.
     No player action needed; it's based on character names.

  3. TWO-WAY SYNC
     The DR sends you recipes you're missing. If you have data
     the DR doesn't, it pulls that from you too. Both sides
     converge.

  4. LIVE UPDATES
     When someone learns a new recipe, a small update is
     broadcast to all online addon users instantly. No full
     re-sync needed.

  5. FAILOVER
     The DR sends a heartbeat every 60 seconds. If 3 are missed,
     the Backup takes over. If both go offline, a new DR is
     elected from the remaining players automatically.

  6. LARGE GUILDS
     Sync data is split into small batches (10 members per
     message) so it never overwhelms WoW's message system,
     even with hundreds of guild members.

The more guildies who install it, the more complete the
guild-wide recipe book becomes.


SLASH COMMANDS
--------------
  /gc            Open/close the window
  /gc minimap    Toggle minimap button visibility
  /gc reset      Wipe all saved data and start fresh
